Nioh


You play a fictionalized version of real-life sailor William Adams, who kills demons in the Sengoku period of Japan. William is sadly underdeveloped, but his adventure through the mountainsides, villages, and hellish battlefields of 1600s Japan is captivating thanks to appearances from other famous historical figures like the mysterious Hattori Hanzo and the fierce Tokugawa Ieyasu.
